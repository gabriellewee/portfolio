  • Details

    Constance needed a reboot of an older, non-responsive site. We worked together to create a fluid layout that resembled the aesthetic of the old website while refreshing the fonts, images, and color choices. The home page includes a full-width slideshow, while individual pages feature unique photographs as backgrounds. Each page has a unique layout depending on the type of information.
