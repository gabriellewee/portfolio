    This website was commissioned by a game studio startup that needed a preview website for their upcoming game. We used CSS image overlays to give headers and images a bit of texture. I collaborated with the lead artist to come up with a logo that reflected the nature of their studio name. I also worked on layouts for a game Kickstarter that showcased art by Micao Penaflor and Kyle Clay.
