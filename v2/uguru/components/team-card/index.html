Uguru Team Card

While each Uguru tutor would have a themed shop card representing their presence on Uguru, the company team members each had a unique card that represented their individuality. Each team member picked a color and a costume for their guru. Jeselle and I designed each card specifically for just one person.

Process

  • Draft 1

    The first draft resembled cards we created for other uses, including the profile. We followed the same information format used in the profile widget.

  • Draft 2

    Later on, Jeselle completely redesigned the team card, with less emphasis on the information and more on the photo. We also decided to make individual guru icons for each team member.

  • Draft 2.5

    Jeselle also ended up making a different style while on the second draft, which utilized a ribbon to delineate the text from the photo. I ended up using elements from both styles for the final draft.

  • Guru Icons

  • Final HTML

    For the final version, I added back more of the information from the first card and simplified the borders. I also changed the colors to contrast better with the team photos.
