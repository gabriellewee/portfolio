Components

Components were designed to be modular and easily usable in a variety of different layouts. Many components have different visual formats based on their category - Academic, Baking, Photography, Tech, or Household. These categories represent the different types of functions covered by the Uguru app and components are styled accordingly.

Jeselle would take the initial wireframes provided and present the team with a first draft in SVG form as well as user personalities, character limits, and color mixes. I would give feedback and - upon consensus - would create the necessary HTML/SASS. The two of us would then animate each component and do some final polishing before completing the project.
