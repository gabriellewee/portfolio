Hayley's portfolio required a creative way to showcase a variety of different animation pieces in an unobtrusive, easy-to-navigate single page format. I designed a floating menu that scrolls smoothly through each part of her website and formatted animated GIFs to take the place of videos.

Her business cards feature key frames from a few of her notable animations for a "ghosting" effect.
