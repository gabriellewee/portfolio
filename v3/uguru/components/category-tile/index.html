Uguru Category Tile

These tiles represented each of the five categories in which Uguru tutors could provide services. Users could select their areas of interest; tutors could select the type of profile shops they wanted to create. These tiles needed to be flexible enough to fit into a variety of different layours while still maintaining similar markup.
